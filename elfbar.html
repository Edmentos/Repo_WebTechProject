Introduction

An Elf Bar is a brand of Disposable Vapes which tend to be slim, stylish and colurful in nature. They come in a wide range of flavour and are designed to fit discretely into anyones jacket or jean pocket. Each device consists of a pre-charged internal battery, pre-filled vape juice and inside contain a coil, tank, and an ergonomic mouthpiece.
